- Networking check fail
  - If it's return false, you can check:
    - Your netline, it's maybe not fully inputed or broken.
    - WiFi(or in china called "WLAN"), Check your driver.
    - Modem(< 56KB/s), You may forgot to dial-up, or you forgot to buy more usage.

- Force set status
  - "netrefresh set <status>"
    - <status> string available: True, true, False, false
